## Overview

This document provides a complete reference for all tutorial lessons in the Handwriting Math app. Each lesson uses high-quality YouTube videos from verified educational channels and follows a structured prerequisite-based progression system.

### Video Sources

All videos are from trusted educational channels:
- **Math Antics** - Engaging visual explanations with animations
- **Math with Mr. J** - Clear, concise step-by-step instructions
- **The Organic Chemistry Tutor** - Detailed problem-solving and applications
- **Khan Academy** - Comprehensive conceptual understanding

### Difficulty Progression

| Difficulty | Description | Problem Unlocking |
|------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **EASY** | Foundational concepts, basic operations | Unlocks after 1+ lesson complete |
| **MEDIUM** | Multi-step problems, combined skills | Unlocks after 2+ lessons complete |
| **HARD** | Complex applications, word problems | Unlocks after all lessons complete |

## Linear Equations

**Total**: 6 lessons (2 EASY, 2 MEDIUM, 2 HARD)
**Focus**: Solving equations with one variable

### LE-1: Introduction to Linear Equations
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-intro`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 11 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Description**: Learn what linear equations are and why they're important. Understand the basic structure of equations with one variable.
- **Key Topics**: Definition, standard form (ax + b = c), why they're called "linear"
- **Video**: Najam Academy

### LE-2: Solving One-Step Equations
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-one-step`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 7 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `linear-equations-intro`
- **Description**: Learn to solve equations that require only one operation.
- **Key Topics**: Inverse operations, addition/subtraction, multiplication/division
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### LE-3: Solving Two-Step Equations
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-two-step`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 9 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `linear-equations-one-step`
- **Description**: Build on one-step equations by learning to solve equations that require two operations.
- **Key Topics**: Order of operations, undoing operations in reverse order (e.g., 2x + 3 = 11)
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### LE-4: Equations with Variables on Both Sides
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-variables-both-sides`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 9 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `linear-equations-two-step`
- **Description**: Learn to solve equations where the variable appears on both sides.
- **Key Topics**: Moving terms, combining like terms, isolating variable (e.g., 3x + 7 = 2x + 15)
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### LE-5: Solving Multi-Step Equations
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-multi-step`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 14 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `linear-equations-variables-both-sides`
- **Description**: Master complex equations with parentheses and multiple operations.
- **Key Topics**: Distributive property, combining like terms, complex equations (e.g., 2(x + 3) = 14)
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### LE-6: Linear Equation Word Problems
- **Slug**: `linear-equations-word-problems`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 11 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `linear-equations-multi-step`
- **Description**: Apply equation-solving skills to real-world scenarios.
- **Key Topics**: Translating words to algebra, setting up equations, problem-solving strategies
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

## Basic Algebra

**Total**: 7 lessons (2 EASY, 3 MEDIUM, 2 HARD)
**Focus**: Algebraic expressions, properties, and simplification

### BA-1: Introduction to Algebra
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-intro`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Description**: Understand what algebra is and learn the basics of variables, constants, and expressions.
- **Key Topics**: Variables, constants, coefficients, expressions vs equations
- **Video**: Math Antics

### BA-2: Combining Like Terms
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-combining-like-terms`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 8 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-intro`
- **Description**: Learn to simplify algebraic expressions by combining terms with the same variable.
- **Key Topics**: Identifying like terms, addition/subtraction of terms, simplification
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### BA-3: The Distributive Property
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-distributive-property`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 9 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-combining-like-terms`
- **Description**: Master the distributive property: a(b + c) = ab + ac.
- **Key Topics**: Distribution over addition/subtraction, reverse distribution (factoring)
- **Video**: Math Antics

### BA-4: Order of Operations in Algebra
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-order-of-operations`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 12 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-distributive-property`
- **Description**: Apply PEMDAS to algebraic expressions.
- **Key Topics**: PEMDAS, nested parentheses, exponents with variables
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

### BA-5: Simplifying Complex Algebraic Expressions
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-simplifying-expressions`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-order-of-operations`
- **Description**: Combine all skills to simplify complex expressions.
- **Key Topics**: Multi-step simplification, combining all learned techniques
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### BA-6: Exponents and Powers
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-exponents`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-simplifying-expressions`
- **Description**: Learn the rules of exponents.
- **Key Topics**: Product rule, quotient rule, power rule, zero/negative exponents
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

### BA-7: Simplifying Algebraic Fractions
- **Slug**: `basic-algebra-fractions`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 11 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `basic-algebra-exponents`
- **Description**: Master operations with algebraic fractions.
- **Key Topics**: Simplification, multiplication/division, common denominators, factoring
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

## Quadratic Equations

**Total**: 7 lessons (1 EASY, 3 MEDIUM, 3 HARD)
**Focus**: Quadratic equations and solution methods

### Q-1: Introduction to Quadratic Equations
- **Slug**: `quadratic-intro`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 8 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Description**: Learn what makes an equation quadratic and understand standard form.
- **Key Topics**: Standard form (ax² + bx + c = 0), squared term, parabolas
- **Video**: Khan Academy

### Q-2: Solving by Square Root Method
- **Slug**: `quadratic-square-root-method`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 7 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-intro`
- **Description**: Solve simple quadratic equations using the square root method.
- **Key Topics**: Square roots, ± notation, positive/negative solutions (x² = k)
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### Q-3: Factoring Quadratic Equations
- **Slug**: `quadratic-factoring`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 14 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-square-root-method`
- **Description**: Learn to solve quadratics by factoring.
- **Key Topics**: Binomials, trinomials, factor pairs, AC method, zero product property
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

### Q-4: Completing the Square
- **Slug**: `quadratic-completing-square`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-factoring`
- **Description**: Master the technique of completing the square.
- **Key Topics**: Rearranging terms, perfect square trinomials, geometric/algebraic perspectives
- **Video**: Khan Academy

### Q-5: The Quadratic Formula
- **Slug**: `quadratic-formula`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 13 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-completing-square`
- **Description**: Learn the quadratic formula: x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / 2a.
- **Key Topics**: Identifying a, b, c; substitution; simplifying radicals
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

### Q-6: Understanding the Discriminant
- **Slug**: `quadratic-discriminant`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 8 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-formula`
- **Description**: Use the discriminant to determine solution types.
- **Key Topics**: b² - 4ac, real vs complex solutions, parabola intersection
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### Q-7: Quadratic Word Problems
- **Slug**: `quadratic-word-problems`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 16 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `quadratic-discriminant`
- **Description**: Apply quadratic equations to real-world scenarios.
- **Key Topics**: Projectile motion, area/perimeter, optimization, setup from word problems
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

## Geometry

**Total**: 6 lessons (2 EASY, 2 MEDIUM, 2 HARD)
**Focus**: Geometric concepts, formulas, and applications

### G-1: Introduction to Geometry
- **Slug**: `geometry-intro`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 8 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Description**: Learn fundamental concepts: points, lines, angles, and basic shapes.
- **Key Topics**: Points, lines, line segments, rays, planes
- **Video**: Math Antics

### G-2: Angles and Angle Relationships
- **Slug**: `geometry-angles`
- **Difficulty**: EASY
- **Duration**: 9 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `geometry-intro`
- **Description**: Understand angle types and relationships.
- **Key Topics**: Acute, right, obtuse, straight angles; complementary, supplementary, vertical angles
- **Video**: Math with Mr. J

### G-3: Perimeter and Area of Rectangles
- **Slug**: `geometry-perimeter-area-rectangles`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 7 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `geometry-angles`
- **Description**: Calculate perimeter and area of rectangles.
- **Key Topics**: Perimeter (2l + 2w), area (l × w), practical applications
- **Video**: Math Antics

### G-4: Area of Triangles and Circles
- **Slug**: `geometry-area-triangles-circles`
- **Difficulty**: MEDIUM
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `geometry-perimeter-area-rectangles`
- **Description**: Learn formulas for triangle and circle area.
- **Key Topics**: Triangle area (½bh), circle area (πr²), formula derivations
- **Video**: Math Antics

### G-5: The Pythagorean Theorem
- **Slug**: `geometry-pythagorean-theorem`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 11 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `geometry-area-triangles-circles`
- **Description**: Master the Pythagorean Theorem for right triangles.
- **Key Topics**: a² + b² = c², finding missing sides, verifying right triangles, applications
- **Video**: The Organic Chemistry Tutor

### G-6: Volume of 3D Shapes
- **Slug**: `geometry-volume-3d-shapes`
- **Difficulty**: HARD
- **Duration**: 9 minutes
- **Prerequisites**: `geometry-pythagorean-theorem`
- **Description**: Calculate volume for 3D shapes.
- **Key Topics**: Prisms (lwh), cylinders (πr²h), spheres (4/3πr³)
- **Video**: Math Antics

Based on completed lessons in each category:

## Maintenance

### Adding New Lessons

When adding new lessons:

1. Follow naming convention: `{category}-{topic}-{descriptor}`
2. Ensure at least one entry point per category (no prerequisites)
3. Update prerequisite chains carefully (avoid circular dependencies)
4. Maintain balanced difficulty distribution (aim for ~30% EASY, ~40% MEDIUM, ~30% HARD)
5. Verify YouTube video is embeddable
6. Keep lessons between 5-15 minutes (ideal)
7. Update this reference document

### Quality Standards

All tutorial videos must meet these criteria:

- ✅ Clear audio (no background noise)
- ✅ Visible demonstrations/annotations
- ✅ Paced for learning (not too fast)
- ✅ Step-by-step progression
- ✅ 5-15 minute duration (preferred)
- ✅ Closed captions available
- ✅ Embeddable on external sites
